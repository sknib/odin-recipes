Lasagna

Description Lasagna is the name of one of the oldest and best-known pasta shapes. It is usually rectangular or ribbon shaped, thicker than tagliatelle, made from a dough based on flour and eggs, with numerous local variants.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1/2 kg bulk Italian sausage
  • 1/2 kg ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 cans crushed tomatoes
  • 2/3 cup water
  • 2-3 T sugar

Steps

  1. 1. Boil pasta: Cook in a large pot of salted water
  2. 2. Prepare meat sauce: Cook sausage and beef with onion and garlic. Drain well, add the pasta sauce & simmer it for a few minutes to thicken.
  3. 3. Combine cheese mixture: Stir the cheese mixture together in a bowl.
  4. 4. Layer & bake: Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with lasagna noodles and bake until browned and bubbly.
